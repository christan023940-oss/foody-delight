Tuna Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 1 can tuna
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp chopped celery
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 slices of bread

Instructions

  1. Drain tuna and mix with mayo and celery.
  2. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Spread mixture on bread slices.
  4. Serve cold or toasted.

Definitions

Mayonnaise
A creamy dressing made from eggs and oil.
Celery
A crunchy vegetable often used in salads.
