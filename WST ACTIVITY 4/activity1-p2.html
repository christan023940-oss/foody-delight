Chicken Adobo

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken thighs
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Black peppercorns

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken in soy sauce and garlic.
  2. Simmer with vinegar, bay leaves, and peppercorns.
  3. Cook until chicken is tender and sauce thickens.
  4. Serve with rice.

Definitions

Adobo
A Filipino dish cooked in vinegar and soy sauce.
Bay Leaf
A fragrant leaf used for seasoning.
