Mango Float

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe mangoes
  • 1 pack graham crackers
  • 1 can condensed milk
  • 1 pack all-purpose cream

Instructions

  1. Slice mangoes thinly.
  2. Mix condensed milk and cream.
  3. Layer graham, cream mixture, and mangoes.
  4. Repeat layers and chill overnight.
  5. Serve cold.

Definitions

Graham Crackers
Sweet crackers used as dessert base.
All-Purpose Cream
A thick dairy cream used in Filipino desserts.
