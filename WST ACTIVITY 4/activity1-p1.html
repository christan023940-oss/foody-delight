Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan
  • Black pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil spaghetti until al dente.
  2. Fry pancetta until crispy.
  3. Beat eggs and mix with Parmesan.
  4. Combine pasta with pancetta and egg mixture.
  5. Season with black pepper and serve.

Definitions

Al dente
Firm to the bite; not overcooked.
Pancetta
Italian cured pork belly.
