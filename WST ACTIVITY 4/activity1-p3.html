Classic Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Whisk egg, milk, and butter in another bowl.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients.
  4. Cook on a hot griddle until golden.
  5. Serve with syrup or fruit.

Definitions

Griddle
A flat surface used for cooking pancakes.
Baking Powder
A leavening agent that helps batter rise.
